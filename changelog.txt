> ## Keybind List HUD (NEW)
> - **New Keybind Display** - A clean on-screen panel that shows all your active keybinds while playing.
> - **Always Visible** - The keybind list stays on screen whenever you have keybinds configured, so you always know what's active.
> - **On/Off Status** - Each keybind shows "On" or "Off" so you can easily see what's currently active at a glance.
> - **Mode Display** - Shows whether each keybind is set to "Hold", "Toggle", or "Always" on the right side.
> - **Matches Your Theme** - The panel automatically uses your menu's accent color, so it matches your watermark and indicators.
> - **Draggable** - You can move the keybind list anywhere on screen by dragging it when the menu is open.
> 
> ## Keybinds Tracked
> - **Legitbot** - Aimbot, Silent Aim, Triggerbot
> - **Visuals** - Third Person
> - **Movement** - Edge Jump, Edge Bug, Fake Duck
