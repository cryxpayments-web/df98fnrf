Legitbot - Aimbot Subtab Rework
New Category-Based UI - Simplified aimbot settings organized by weapon category (Pistol, Rifle, SMG, Sniper, Shotgun, LMG).

Share Across Categories - Toggle to use same settings for all weapons, or customize per-category.
Per-Category Settings - FOV, Smoothing, Hitbox, RCS, and Assisted Recoil now configurable per weapon type.
Cleaner Layout - Removed confusing "override" system, settings apply directly to selected category.
Config Compatible - Existing configs will load correctly with new system.
