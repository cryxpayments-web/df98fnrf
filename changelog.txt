> **changelog:** `07/01/2026 - 8:09 PM [GMT+01:00]`

> ## C4 / Bomb ESP
> - **New C4 World Indicator** - When the bomb is planted, a clean circular indicator appears above it showing the countdown timer.
> - **Progress Ring** - Visual ring shows how much time is left at a glance. Changes color as time runs out (accent → orange → red).
> - **Bomb Icon** - Displays a bomb icon inside the indicator for easy recognition.
> - **Menu Options** - Toggle the C4 ESP, timer display, distance, site info, and defuse info separately in the Visuals menu.
> 
> ## Radar
> - **Circular Radar** - Radar now displays as a clean circle with Reev accent colors.
> - **Stability Fix** - Fixed a crash that could occur when loading the radar map.
